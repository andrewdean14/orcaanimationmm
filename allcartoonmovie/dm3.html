Despicable Me 3 (2017)

Married Anti-Villain League (AVL) agents Gru and Lucy Wilde are sent to stop Balthazar Bratt from stealing the Dumont diamond.
Gru recovers the diamond, but Bratt escapes. Displeased, Director Valerie Da Vinci, taking former Director Silas Ramsbottom's place, dismisses Gru and Lucy from the AVL.
