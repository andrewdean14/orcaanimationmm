Attack On Titan Season 2 (2018)

After fighting the Female Titan, Eren finds no time to rest as a horde of Titans is approaching Wall Rose and the battle for humanity continues.
As the Survey Corps races to save the wall, they uncover more about the invading Titans and the dark secrets of their own members.
